A little bit about me..

My name is Emma Haeussler. I am from Fort Worth, Texas. I am a creative person. I have always enjoyed the arts, music, and literature; I like to use my creativity to solve complex problems and to build connections that form larger pictures. It may come, then, as no surprise that I chose to study both Math and Computing at Texas A&M, where I am currently a student. I have tremendously enjoyed studying both content areas, especially getting to witness the myriad connections between the two.

I hope to one day work as a software engineer and use my knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems that ultimately help people. Some of my strengths that I believe would positively contribute to a software engineering team are that I am dedicated to the tasks I undertake and seek excellence in all I do. With the tasks I undertake however, I seek improvement in how I undertake them, and not perfection, and extend that same grace towards others. I have strong communication skills to clearly be able to articulate the problems and solutions that I deal with to others, and I enjoy collaborating in teams to achieve our goals.

Some fun facts about me, I am a member of the Hullabaloo Pep Band at Texas A&M. (I play the euphonium.) We support the volleyball, soccer, and basketball teams on campus. I also love Jesus, coffee, and learning new things.
